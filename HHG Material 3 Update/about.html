About Hot House Grillé

Our Story

Hot House Grillé was born from a passion for authentic Southern cooking and a dream to create a welcoming space for the Sumiton community. Opened in October 2024, we’re excited to share our favorite family recipes and create new memories with you. We are a Proudly Veteran-Owned Business, committed to serving those who serve and our entire community with dedication and heart.

We believe in fresh ingredients, hearty portions, and service with a smile. Whether you’re joining us for a quick lunch, a family dinner, or grabbing a to-go meal, we aim to make every experience at Hot House Grillé a delightful one.

What We Offer

  • Delicious Southern restaurant & café fare
  • Comfortable indoor dining
  • Outdoor seating to enjoy Alabama weather
  • Kid-friendly menu
  • Easy dine-in and take-out options
  • A warm welcome for everyone—including our local heroes!
